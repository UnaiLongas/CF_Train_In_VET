CF Train in VET

CF Train in VET is a project whose main objective is to train
future professionals in sustainable skills based on the concept
of Carbon Footprint calculation and reduction.






OBJECTIVES

Objectives

Carbon Footprint Calculation
Train students and staff in Carbon Footprint calculation.
Emissions Reduction
Reduce 30% of school emissions
Students and staff with green skills
Raise school-wide awareness on climate change
Share good practices
Get students to share good practices at an international level.
Social Inclusion And Gender Equality
Involve 100 % of students with special needs in the project
Involve companies and schools
Involve all the students and partners in the project(CF reduction and calculation)
New policies and plans
Design school environmental policies and sustainability plans
