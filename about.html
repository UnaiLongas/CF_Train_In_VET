CF Train in VET

Vocational Education and Training (VET) plays a key role in providing students with innovative responses to the effects of climate change and supporting the transition to sustainable development and green economy. The partner organizations of “CFTrain in VET” want to become carbon neutral models through collaboration and sharing good practices.
